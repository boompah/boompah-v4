Twiddy Homepage Redesign

by Ryan Bollenbach

This project is intended to be viewed on a desktop/laptop screen at 72ppi.

Objective

The purpose of this project was to come up with ideas to improve the Twiddy & Company homepage UX and make it easier for guests find and book homes.

Twiddy & Company is a Outer Banks vacation home rental management firm based in Duck, NC and has been in business since 1978.

Simplify Search

The current design allows guests to select from high level categories, a quick search module, browse and advanced search.

This redesign places a simple search module in the center of the page with the category tiles presented below the featured homes. A guest can simply fill out the quick search and refine as needed with advanced perameters once the basic details are entered.

Story Telling

The current website has these interesting facts on the about page. The "Where it all began" module introduces guests with a friendly hello, adding integrity.

Social Proof

After reviewing Twiddy's social media presence, I discovered they have over 32,000 followers on Facebook and nearly 700 reviews, 497 of which are 5 stars. I added three reviews so guests can easily discover what customers are saying about Twiddy.

Footer

I added a footer to provide the user with an additional way to navigate the site alongside a call to action for social media pages.

Conslusion

These changes will help better tell the brand's story at a glance, simplify the search process and provide social proof in regards to customer service.
